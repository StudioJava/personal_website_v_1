BIO

My name is Kye and music is my life. I started playing drums when I was four, and moved to guitar and harmonica at the age of 12. By the time I was 17, there was no doubt in my mind about what I wanted from my life. To make music. I moved to New York and lived my dream, serving coffee by day and playing clubs by night. I signed my first album deal at just 21 years old - it all seemed too good to be true, but it has only gotten better. For more about me, listen to my music. It says it all..
